
RULES FOR APOCALYPSE
1) Pieces move the same as in regular chess
2) Capture all opponents pawns to win
3) Movement is simultaneous and declared at the start of turn
4) Pieces can try to move to the same location
  - Pieces of same type moving to same square annihilate
  - Pieces of different type, pawn is annihilated
5) Pieces that try to capture each other simultaneously swap places
6) If last pawns annihilate simultaneously its a draw
7) If no moves are possible the game is a stalemate
